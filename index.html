license inclusion

Each @s9a repo includes an opensource license. We mix it up per repo among these licenses that best support inclusive ethos.

Zlib zlib license is ideal for our libraries and core content. it's a smooth permissive gender neutral license with broad recognition and optional attribution. like example or template
Unlicense we choose the unlicense for our public domain contributions like our terms and free templates. the unlicense has universal reach with inclusive wording and rebellious appeal.
Ms-PL Our interweb template includes Ms-PL as a basic balanced default public license. it's anonymous, gender neutral, and industry safe.
MIT-0
0BSD
MIT-0 has broad permissive wording and industry appeal. MIT is popular with wombats. Use zero to stay humble or apply MIT to advocate a brand like we do.

UPL-1.0 Universal Permissive License is complex but has an inspiring name and balances MITish features with universal wording.
MPL-2.0 MPL is extra safe, anonymous, gender neutral, loosely copyleft, and foxy because we advocate Mozilla.
EPL-2.0 EPL is much like MPL but with lunar energy and suitable when emotionally desired.

These licenses provide a range of features. Trademarks aren't affected by them. All of them are gender neutral. Some are anonymous. Some spread our brand. Each repo should have one. If missing please ask us thru an issue or twitter :)
